Open Data Day Croatia 2021

Online

Hosts: Gong & Code for Croatia | RSVP: panel & hackathon

Open Data Day is a weekend of civic innovation and social activism through open data work, which is celebrated globally from March 5 to 7, 2021. Join this year's Open Data Days!

Virtual Conference

Web streaming from Journalists house in Zagreb

Friday, March 5, 2021, at 10 a.m.

Ask what you want to know about the amendments to the Right to Access Information Act that will take effect in July this year. Members of the recently formed working group to amend the Act live will answer your questions about the right to access information. The next panel will be dedicated to the discussion on how open data can help the community due to disasters that have digitized society. Register online participation.

Panel info

Virtual Weekend Hackathon

Join online from your home

Saturday and Sunday, March 6th and 7th, 2021 at 10 a.m.

Be part of the team that develops Popravi. to - a free service for reporting problems and damage to local public infrastructure, join the creation of a central data repository for the coronavirus, research data collected by Parlametar on the work of MPs, or suggest a set you would like to analyze or your own project. If you want to know more about the Code for the Croatia team and how to join them - Lana, Miroslav, and Filip will answer all your questions about civilian hacking and how to get involved even if you are not a hacker. Register for online participation.
